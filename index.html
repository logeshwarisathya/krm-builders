About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Property & Construction

With over 15 years of experience, KRM Planner and Builders & Services has been the go-to firm for hundreds of families and investors across Tamil Nadu for all their real estate and construction needs.

  • Government-recognized expertise in DTCP approvals, ensuring your property plans meet all legal standards.

  • Advanced 2D & 3D design capabilities with experienced architects and draughtsmen for precise layouts.

  • Complete land registration support — hassle-free documentation and sub-registrar liaison services.

  • End-to-end project management from initial planning to final handover with full transparency.
